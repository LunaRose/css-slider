Rainbow Fudge

This delicious fudge was made with a recipe I found for free online. It has proven very popular at parties and gaming. Just watch out, if you leave it out of the fridge it doesnt go off but the salt sweats out and leave a really salty initial taste.

  • Time to make: 1hr
  • Date made: 4th July
  • Reception: Shock and Awe!

Rainbow Unicorn Piñata Cookies

Wow! These are all kinds of awesome. They take soooo long to make but are so worth it. You can find the recipe here. My ones had M&M minis inside but you can put anything you want in, I found a place that sells little sugar internal organs so my next batch will have those and strawberry jam inside :D

  • Time to Make: About a day for 12
  • Date Made: Somtime in 2012 I think
  • Reception: Utter astonishment

Awesome Cupcakes

I made these colourful cupcakes one Easter to take into work. The case is made out of white chocolate, the icing is rainbow butter-cream Earl Gray and the cupcakes were Lady Gray flavoured. I also made these for my Grandfather's 70th birthday.

  • Time to Make: 1-2 hours
  • Date Made: Easter 2012
  • Reception: These always go down well

Melted Snowman Cookies

These were a fun secret santa I gave to somebody a few years ago. They are super easy to make and you can use almost any cookie recipe for the base. As you can see I didn't get the arms quite right but they are still fun and tasty.

  • Time to Make: About an hour (detail depending)
  • Date Made: Xmas 2012
  • Reception: I couldn't ask.... secret santa :(
